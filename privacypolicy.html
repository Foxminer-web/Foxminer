Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 02, 2025

1. Introduction

At Foxminer, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our cryptocurrency mining application (“App”). By using Foxminer, you consent to the practices described here.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Email address and Monero (XMR) wallet address when you sign up or save a wallet.
  • Usage Data: Hashrate, worker stats, and mining activity, linked to your wallet via MoneroOcean.
  • Device Data: IP address, operating system, and device type (e.g., macOS ARM64) for analytics and support.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Deliver mining services and display stats on your dashboard.
  • Process payouts through MoneroOcean (0.003 XMR fee applies).
  • Improve the App via anonymized usage analytics.
  • Contact you for support or updates (email only, opt-out available).

4. Sharing Your Information

We don’t sell your data—period. We share only what’s needed:

  • MoneroOcean: Wallet address and mining data for pool operations.
  • Service Providers: Anonymized data with analytics or hosting partners (e.g., Render).
  • Legal Requests: If required by law, we’ll comply after notifying you where possible.

5. Data Security

We use encryption and secure servers to protect your data, but no system’s 100% hack-proof. You’re responsible for keeping your email and wallet credentials safe.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Update your wallet or email via the App.
  • Request data deletion by emailing [support@foxminer.com]—we’ll wipe what we can, but MoneroOcean data stays per their policy.
  • Opt out of emails anytime—hit “unsubscribe” in any message.

7. Cookies

No cookies in the App itself, but our server (Render) may use session cookies for login. Clear your cache to ditch ‘em.

8. Changes

We’ll update this policy as needed—check back here. Continued use means you’re cool with the changes.

9. Contact Us

Questions? Hit us at [support@foxminer.com].
