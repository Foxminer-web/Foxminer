About FOXMINER v1.0

Mining Monero, Made Dead Simple
FOXMINER v1.0 is the world’s first frontend CPU miner for Monero (XMR), built to shred traditional mining setups. Forget downloading configs, wrestling with pools, or tweaking command lines—our app drops you straight into the action. Install it, sign up, and start hashing XMR with RandomX under the hood, all in under a minute. We’re not reinventing the algo; we’re making effortless and opening the door for new users to onboard into the mining ecosystem.
Launched in 2025 after 18 months of grinding, FOXMINER turns the stale RandomX game into a profit machine for anyone with a Mac ARM64 rig. Monero mining’s been a slog since 2019—pools, setups, and shitty yields killed the vibe. We said fuck that, built a slick frontend, and tied it to MoneroOcean for seamless payouts. This isn’t just another miner—it’s the XMR gateway no one else dared to forge.
Zero setup—just click, sign up, and mine XMR instantly.
Powered by RandomX, optimized for ARM64 muscle (M1/M2).
Hassle-free pool integration with MoneroOcean—auto-payouts at 0.3 XMR.
First-ever XMR frontend—mining’s never been this clean and mean.
Lightweight and lean—max hashes, minimal resource drain.
