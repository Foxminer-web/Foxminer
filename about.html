About FOXMINER v1.0

Mining Monero, Made Simple
FOXMINER v1.0 introduces the first frontend CPU miner for Monero (XMR), designed to eliminate the complexity of traditional mining. No more pool configurations, manual downloads, or technical hurdles—our app streamlines the process so you can install, sign up, and start mining in moments. Built on the proven RandomX algorithm and enhanced with auto-algo switching, FOXMINER maximizes your CPU rewards while reducing strain compared to conventional miners.
Launched in 2025 after 18 months of dedicated development, FOXMINER transforms Monero mining into an accessible and efficient experience for Mac ARM64 users. Since 2019, CPU mining has struggled with cumbersome setups and diminishing returns—our solution changes that. Integrated with MoneroOcean and featuring auto-algo switching, we optimize profitability with minimal resource impact, setting a new standard as the pioneering XMR frontend miner.
Effortless setup—install, sign up, and mine XMR in under a minute.
Leverages RandomX with auto-algo switching for peak CPU efficiency.
Seamless MoneroOcean integration—automatic payouts at 0.3 XMR.
First-of-its-kind frontend miner—simplicity meets innovation.
Optimized for ARM64 (M1/M2)—high yields, low resource usage.
