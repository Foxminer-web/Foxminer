How It Works

FOXMINER makes mining Monero (XMR) simple and efficient with two powerful options: CPU Mining via our lightweight executable for macOS and Linux, or Web Mining directly in your browser. Download our streamlined package for macOS (DMG) or Linux (tar.gz), or jump into mining instantly on Safari or Chrome. Monitor your progress in real-time through the Terminal output or our detailed web dashboard—no complex setup required.

CPU Mining

Our CPU Mining solution is built for simplicity and performance, supporting macOS ARM64 and Linux ARM64, with Windows and additional platforms coming in Q4 2025. Download the DMG (macOS) or tar.gz (Linux) package, which includes the executable, config.json, and README. Launch the executable in Terminal, and you’re mining in seconds. Track basic stats like hashrate and accepted shares directly in the Terminal, or visit https://foxminer-web.github.io/Foxminer/, sign up, enter your XMR wallet address, click “Save,” and access our detailed mining dashboard for in-depth stats like earnings and worker performance. The executable runs efficiently in the background, optimized for minimal resource usage, and continues mining until you stop it or restart your system.

  • Quick Setup—download, extract, and run the executable in under a minute.
  • Terminal Stats—view hashrate, accepted shares, and settings instantly.
  • Web Dashboard—sign up and enter your wallet address for detailed mining insights.
  • Resource Efficient—maximizes hashrate with low CPU and RAM usage.
  • Background Mining—runs seamlessly until stopped or system restarts.
  • macOS & Linux Support—ARM64 optimized, with more platforms in Q4 2025.
  • Free to Use—no upfront costs, just pure mining power.
