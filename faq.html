Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Foxminer?

Foxminer is a free app to mine Monero (XMR) using your CPU—easy setup, no pool config needed. Starts with macOS ARM64, more platforms Q4 2025.

2. How do I start mining?

Sign up, enter your XMR wallet address (or create one in-app), hit “Save,” then “Start Mining.” Done—watch it roll!

3. What’s the 5% dev fee?

5% of your hashrate supports us—3% to Foxminer for servers, 2% to the original coder. Keeps it free for you.

4. What’s the 0.003 XMR pool fee?

MoneroOcean charges 0.003 XMR per payout for their auto-algo switching—hits when you cash out at 0.3 XMR.

5. Why’s my hashrate low?

Could be CPU load, background apps, or network lag. Check your rig, close extras, and ensure a solid connection.

6. Can I mine on multiple devices?

Yep—install Foxminer on each, use the same wallet, and stats stack on your dashboard.

7. How often do I get paid?

Auto-payouts hit at 0.3 XMR via MoneroOcean—depends on your hashrate and network difficulty.

8. Does mining hurt my computer?

We optimize for low strain, but heavy use can wear hardware over time—your risk, keep it cool.

9. What if I lose my wallet?

Your XMR is tied to your wallet address—lose access, lose funds. Back it up, we can’t recover it.

10. How do I stop mining?

Hit “Stop Mining” in the app or reboot your device—it runs in the background till you say no.
