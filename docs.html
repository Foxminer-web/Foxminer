Introduction

Our lightweight executable, powered by RandomX and integrated with MoneroOcean’s auto-algo switching, maximizes your CPU’s hashrate while keeping resource usage low. Run it in Terminal, monitor stats instantly, or dive into detailed insights on our web dashboard—no fuss, just results.

Getting Started

Starting with FOXMINER is simple. Download the DMG for macOS or tar.gz for Linux from our Installers page. Extract the package, which includes the executable, config.json, and README. Run the executable in Terminal, enter your Monero wallet address (44-88 characters, starting with 4 or 8) when prompted, and mining begins within seconds. Watch stats like hashrate and accepted shares update live in Terminal, or visit https://foxminer-web.github.io/Foxminer/, sign up, enter your wallet address, click “Save,” and access our detailed dashboard for deeper insights like earnings and worker stats. No account is required to mine, but signing up unlocks the dashboard.

Technical Details

FOXMINER’s open-source RandomX engine is compiled for macOS ARM64, Intel, and Linux ARM64, delivering 1-3 kH/s on M1/M2 Macs (e.g., 2.5 kH/s on M2 Max, 16 cores) and 0.5-1.5 kH/s on Intel i7/i9 or Linux rigs. It connects to MoneroOcean’s pool with a 0.3 XMR payout threshold (configurable) and a 0.003 XMR pool fee. Our 5% dev fee (3% to FOXMINER, 2% to the original coder) is far lower than competitors’ 20-30%. Expect 1 XMR per 20-40 MH of shares (difficulty ~120G, 1% orphan rate). Payouts are automatic via MoneroOcean to your wallet. Stats update in Terminal instantly, with detailed metrics available on our web dashboard after signing up.

Troubleshooting

Mining won’t start? Ensure your wallet address is valid (44-88 chars, starts with 4 or 8). Re-enter in the Terminal prompt.

Low hashrate? Older hardware or fewer cores reduce output—try a high-end M1/M2 or Linux ARM64 system.

Executable crashes? Verify macOS (14.0+ recommended) or Linux compatibility. Reinstall and follow README instructions.

Dashboard empty? Sign up and enter your wallet at https://foxminer-web.github.io/Foxminer/ to sync stats.
