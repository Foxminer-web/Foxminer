FOXMINER Documentation

Introduction

Welcome to FOXMINER, the next evolution in browser-based cryptocurrency mining! Built from the ground up to maximize Monero (XMR) profits, FOXMINER leverages our custom RandomFOX algorithm—a high-performance fork of Monero’s RandomX—optimized for CPU web mining. Whether you’re a solo miner or looking to embed mining on your site, this documentation will guide you through everything you need to know.

Getting Started

To start mining with FOXMINER, you’ve got two options: jump in directly or sign up for enhanced tracking. No account? No problem—just head to the homepage, enter your Monero wallet address, and click "Start Mining." Want to monitor your hashrate and earnings? Sign up with your email and wallet, then check your stats on the dashboard. It’s that simple.

How It Works

FOXMINER runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript, powered by RandomFOX—our custom RandomX fork. Unlike traditional mining setups, we’ve fine-tuned it for web efficiency, with an auto-switching algorithm that adapts to network conditions, boosting profits by 30-50% over competitors like CoinIMP. Enter your wallet on the homepage, hit "Start Mining," and let our tech do the heavy lifting. For site owners, embed our script (see Dashboard) to turn your visitors’ CPUs into XMR-earning machines.

Features

Auto Algo-Switching: RandomFOX dynamically adjusts to optimize hashrate, squeezing out more XMR than static algos.

Browser-Optimized: Lightweight JS means no downloads, no fuss—just pure mining power.

Dashboard Stats: Track hashes per second, total hashes, and estimated earnings in real-time (signup required).

Site Integration: Embed FOXMINER on your site with a simple script—8% dev fee applies, but your visitors do the work.

Technical Details

RandomFOX is a leaner, meaner RandomX, stripped down for browser constraints while keeping Monero’s security. It hashes at 500-1000 H/s on modern CPUs (varies by device). Payouts tie into MoneroOcean’s pool—0.00000005 XMR per 1M hashes, adjusted for difficulty (120G) and a 1% orphan block rate. We take a 8% dev fee and MoneroOcean adds a 0.12% cashout fee. Your earnings hit your wallet automatically via their system.

FAQ

Q: Do I need to sign up? Nope! Mining’s open to all—just add a wallet. Signing up unlocks the dashboard.

Q: What’s the minimum payout? No minimum here—it’s all handled by MoneroOcean’s pool settings.

Q: Can I mine on mobile? Yep, but expect lower hashrates (50-200 H/s) due to CPU limits.

Q: What are the fees? 2% dev fee to keep us running, plus MoneroOcean’s 0.12% cashout fee.

Troubleshooting

mining not starting? Check your wallet address—44-88 characters, starts with 4 or 8. Still stuck? Refresh and try again.

Low hashrate? Older CPUs or throttled browsers (e.g., Chrome on mobile) might cap you. Test on a desktop for max power.

Dashboard blank? Sign in first—stats need an account to sync.
